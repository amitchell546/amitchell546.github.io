This website is a review and comparison of these three web hosting providers:

  • bluehost
  • HostGator
  • SITEBUILDER

1. bluehost

Blue host was founded in 1996 and has quickly become a very popular webhost provider

with over 2,000,000 websites being hosted through them. They have very impressive

and can boast an uptime over 99.99%, an average loading time of less than 424ms, and

very competitive pricing, starting as low as just $2.75 a month. However a website this

must come with some drawbacks, according to user reviews, while their customer support

is very fast they lack knowledge on more technical issues. In addition, their $2.75 a

month price tag is only available with a 3 year long contract.

2. HostGator

HostGator was founded in 2003 in a the florida dorm room of Brent Oxley. Since it's

conception it has become one of the most recognizable names in webhosting. With uptimes

that are .04% higher than the industry standard as well as comprehensive customer support.

However, their loading times are not great, falling 10% slower in loading times compared

to competitors. They also have the same lengthy contract you must sign on with if you want

a good deal.

3. SITEBUILDER

SITEBUILDER is a free website hosting service with a built in website designer. They

sadly do not offer all of their services entirely free, in exchange for their hosting.,

ads run on your page, which isn't that big of a deal being that many web hosting options

require lengthy paid contracts. However they have some of the most atrocious customer

service, with unhelpful and outsourced personnel. They also throttle loading speeds for

free users, if you want higher load times you have to upgrade from their free version.
